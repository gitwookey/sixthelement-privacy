Privacy Policy The Sixth Element: Fitness RPG · Last updated: 12 July 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application The Sixth Element: Fitness RPG ("the App") handles your information. The App is a personal habit and fitness tracker. It is developed and maintained by an individual developer ("we", "us"). If you have any questions, contact us at wookey420@gmail.com. Summary The App is local-first: your data is stored only on your device. There is no user account and no server operated by us. We never receive or store your data. We use no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking, and we do not sell or share your data. The App contacts a few public third-party services only to look up information you request (product barcodes, food search, map tiles, moon phase). These queries do not include your personal data or any account identity. You can delete all of your data at any time by using the in-app reset or by uninstalling the App. Data stored on your device All of the following is saved locally on your device (in the app's local storage) and is never transmitted to us: Activity logs, skills, experience points, levels, ranks, streaks and achievements. Habits and habit history. Body statistics you choose to enter (e.g. height, weight over time, and a gender setting used for figures). Nutrition entries, meals and food library. GPS run/route data recorded with the run tracker. Optional voice notes you record during a run (stored as audio files on your device). Avatar customisation, reminders and planned items. Backup files: the App periodically saves an automatic backup, and lets you export a manual backup, as JSON files. Automatic backups are written to your device's Documents folder; a manual export is passed to the system share sheet so you can save it wherever you choose. Because this data lives on your device, it is your responsibility to keep it safe. Resetting the App from within it, or uninstalling the App, removes the data held inside the App itself. However, backup files are not removed automatically: any automatic backups saved to your Documents folder, and any manual backups you have exported or shared elsewhere, remain on your device (or wherever you saved them) until you delete those files yourself using your device's file manager. Device permissions and why they are used Location (fine & coarse) — used only when you actively track a run, to record your route. Routes are stored on your device and are not sent to us. Camera — used to scan product barcodes for nutrition lookup. Images are processed on-device and are not stored or transmitted. Microphone — used only if you choose to record a voice note during a run. Recordings are stored on your device. Notifications & exact alarms — used to deliver the reminders you set at the times you choose. Calendar (read & write) — used only if you enable calendar sync, to create/update your planned items in a dedicated calendar on your device. Internet — used only to contact the third-party lookup and map services listed below. Third-party services The App sends limited, non-personal queries to the following public services. We do not send your name, account, location history, or any on-device data to them. As with any internet request, these services receive standard technical information such as your IP address, governed by their own privacy policies. Open Food Facts — barcode and food search. Data sent: a barcode number. Privacy policy FarmSense — moon phase lookup. Data sent: a calendar date. Website OpenStreetMap — map tiles for the run tracker. Privacy policy Children The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. Data retention and deletion We do not hold any of your data, so there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our side. Data held inside the App stays on your device until you delete it by resetting or uninstalling the App. Backup files (automatic backups in your Documents folder, or manual backups you exported or shared) are kept separately and remain until you delete them yourself. Changes to this policy We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
